A California company with a global
perspective, Rivian builds all-electric
vehicles with a common goal — preserving
the natural world for generations to come.

Our vehicles are always
evolving

Whether taking families on new adventures or
electrifying fleets at scale, our technology-led
vehicles offer a fully connected experience that
improves over time. Our in-vehicle software and app
work seamlessly together to help you get where
you're going safely and in comfort.


Our mission is forever

We're here to preserve the planet for our kids and our kids' kids.
It's a huge objective — but one we believe is possible when
individuals and entire industries come together to create
fundamental change. By building uncompromising products for
exploring the world, we make it easy to join us and make a
difference.
